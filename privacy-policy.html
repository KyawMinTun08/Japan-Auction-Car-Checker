Privacy Policy

Last updated: 22 July 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Japan Auction Car Checker (JACC) handles information when you use the JACC website, Android application, Telegram services, and member features.

Information We May Process

Depending on the feature you use, JACC may process:

  • Telegram user ID and username.
  • Membership start date, expiry date, package, status, and login credentials.
  • Vehicle search details, model names, chassis information, and auction-related requests.
  • Basic technical information needed to operate the website and app, such as browser or device information.

How We Use Information

Information is used only to:

  • Provide member login and premium access.
  • Operate vehicle checking, auction, model, price, and chassis features.
  • Manage subscriptions, expiry dates, and customer support.
  • Maintain security, prevent abuse, and improve service reliability.

Storage and Third-Party Services

JACC may use third-party infrastructure such as GitHub Pages, Google Apps Script, Google Sheets, Railway, Telegram, and related hosting or automation services. These providers may process limited technical data according to their own privacy policies.

Data Sharing

JACC does not sell personal information. Information may only be shared when necessary to provide the service, comply with legal obligations, protect users, or prevent fraud and abuse.

Data Retention

Membership and account information may be kept for as long as needed to operate the service, maintain records, resolve disputes, and enforce access rules. Data may be deleted or anonymised when it is no longer required.

Security

Reasonable technical and organisational measures are used to protect account and membership information. However, no online service can guarantee absolute security.

Children's Privacy

JACC is a vehicle information and auction support service and is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Your Choices

You may contact JACC to request access, correction, or deletion of personal information associated with your membership, subject to legal and operational requirements.

Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated when JACC features, services, or legal requirements change. The latest version will be published on this page.

Contact

For privacy questions or requests, contact JACC through the official Japan Auction Car Checker Telegram service or the official website.
